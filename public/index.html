What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber trick where scammers pretend to be a trusted organization—like your Bank, College, or Google. They copy official logos and designs to make their fake emails look exactly like real ones.

Their goal is to fool you into clicking a link or downloading a file.

  • 📩 They send fake emails that look real.
  • 😱 They try to scare you so you click fast.
  • 💰 They want to steal your password or money.
